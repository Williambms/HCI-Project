About Us

Meet Our Founder

John Davidson was born in July 16th 1865, London, United
Kingdom. He became an assistant bookeper at age 16 and went
into several business partnerships beginning at age 20. On 1896
he took interest on chocolate as it became more popular and the
demand is really high. He began researching how to make the
best chocolate in the world. After two years of trials and errors he
invented a new way of making chocolate.

Our First Factory

Created in Peterborough, Great Britain on February 10th 1902. Our
first team consist of 30 people and can produced up to 3300
chocolate per day. In two year our sales increased rapidly, so in
order to meet the demand this factory got renovation and added
more machinery and more worker. And our daily production can
make up to 12000 chocolate per day. Three years later we create
our second factory in Birmingham and the rest is history.
